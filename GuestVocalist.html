AN EVENING FOR JACK

A Tribute to Jack Bruce | Shepherds Bush Empire, London | 25/10/2016

In October 2016 Emma was invited by musical directors Malcolm Bruce & Pete Brown to sing as part of the house band at "An Evening For Jack", a night of music held at Shepherds Bush Empire, London on 25th October 2016, to celebrate the life and music of Jack Bruce. Some of the other musicians performing were:

  • Ginger Baker
  • Dennis Chambers
  • Bernie Marsden
  • Terry Reid
  • Steve Hackett
  • Mick Taylor
  • David Sancious

The concert is being made into a DVD but in the meantime you can click here to find some amazing footage of the concert.

MARTYN JOSEPH

BACKING VOCAL : SINGLE : "Let's talk about it in the morning"

From the self-titled album Martyn Joseph & produced by Mick Glossop whose previous work included albums with The Waterboys & 29 Palms. The first single from Martyn Joseph, "Talk about it in the Morning" co-written with Tom Robinson was released in 1995 and reached the Top 50 charts in the UK and features Emma Wilson on backing vocals.
