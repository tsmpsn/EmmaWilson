BRITISH BLUES SIREN EMMA WILSON HAILS FROM TEESSIDE - THE HOME OF MANY GREAT MUSICAL ARTISTS. EMMA HAS A FABULOUS & DYNAMIC VOICE, MOVING EFFORTLESSLY FROM HEARTWRENCHING SWEET SOUL LICKS TO POWERFUL REAL BLUES. ON RECORD EMMA'S TONE AND DELIVERY IS GORGEOUS, BEING TENDER AND UNIQUE - WITH SHADES OF EARLY ARETHA FRANKLIN & LOVELY REFERENCES TO EMMA'S OTHER SOUL ICON ANN PEEBLES. EMMA'S LIVE SHOW IS VIBRANT & VISCERAL, SHE FRONTS HER BAND WITH TRUE PASSION AND GIVES HER ALL AT EVERY PERFORMANCE, ALWAYS LEAVING HER HEART ON THE STAGE. EMMA AND HER BAND HAVE PERFORMED AT FESTIVALS AND BLUES BARS ACROSS THE UK SUCH AS EALING BLUES FESTIVAL,THE CRAWDADDY CLUB,RICHMOND,THE LEGENDARY EALING CLUB, HOWZAT BLUES FESTIVAL AS WELL AS TONNES OF BRILLIANT BLUES & SOUL NIGHTS AROUND THE COUNTRY.. BUILDING UP THEIR AUDIENCE. EMMA'S FOLLOWING HAS GROWN THROUGH HER INCREDIBLE PERFORMANCES ONSTAGE - AS WELL AS HER HER EFFORT OFFSTAGE TO BE INVOLVED IN ALL ASPECTS OF THE BLUES & SOUL SCENE, PERSONALLY TALKING TO AND ENGAGING WITH DJs, PHOTOGRAPHERS & PROMOTERS & ALWAYS FINDING TIME TO TALK TO FANS - SHE HAS BECOME AN EXCITING AND WELCOMED ADDITION TO THE BRITISH BLUES & SOUL SCENE. EMMA'S PROFILE HAS RISEN DRAMATICALLY IN THE LAST 18 MONTHS, HER GREAT VOICE COMBINED WITH HER PERSONAL CONNECTION TO THE PEOPLE WHO REALLY MAKE THE SCENE TICK HAS EARNED HER DOZENS OF RADIO INTERVIEWS, 100'S OF RADIO PLAYS - ACROSS THE WORLD - AWARDS AND ACCOLADES FROM RADIO STATIONS AND MAGAZINES AND SUPERB PRESS. EMMA IS ALSO A MUCH COVETED GUEST SINGER AND WAS PART OF THE SUPERGROUP AT "AN EVENING FOR JACK", A TRIBUTE CONCERT TO JACK BRUCE, SHEPHERDS BUSH EMPIRE OCTOBER 2016. THE BAND ALSO FEATURED BERNIE MARSDEN, TERRY REID, MICK TAYLOR, STEVE HACKETT & DENNIS CHAMBERS..PLUS MANY MORE GREAT AND GOOD OF THE MUSIC SCENE.
