Emma Wilson | Singer & Actor

Blues & Soul Singer Emma Wilson has a big fabulous voice and works her great band to the max with her vibrant on-stage persona, moving from heart-wrenching sweet soul licks to powerful blues, her live show is emotional and cool. Emma Wilson Blues Band are touring and recording , All live dates are listed here!

Emma has become the go to singer for bands looking for strong and soulful vocals. Emma has performed and recorded (see Guest Vocalist) with many other great artists and was invited by Musical Directors Malcolm Bruce & Pete Brown to be a featured singer in the Supergroup Band for "An Evening For Jack (Bruce)" at Shepherds Bush Empire London 2016 (which also featured Ginger Baker, Bernie Marsden, Terry Reid, Mick Taylor, Corky Laing, John Altman and many more). Some other great names Emma has performed with include- Elliot Randall, Pete Brown, Pig Iron, Will Johns, 29 Palms, Martyn Joseph, Steve Rodgers & Malcolm Bruce.

Emma is honoured to front "The Fabulosa Rhythm & Blues Big Band" an amazing collective of the North East's finest musicians brought together by two legendary Teesside musicians and record shop proprietors Ottie (Austin) McLaughlin and Alan Fearnley. Look out for exclusive gigs on the Dates page.

Emma also works as an Actor & Model and is signed to Ugly Models (London).
